Introduction

Rock, Paper, Scissors is a simple, timeless game that almost everyone knows. Our goal is to bring people togesther through a series of such easy-to-play games, allowing users to engage in lighthearted competition without a steep learning curve.

Vision

Our platform's core idea is to offer accessible social engagement through fun and simple games. Over time, we aim to expand this concept into a suite of casual games that foster connection and joy, providing a variety of experiences for players to enjoy together.

Why Blockchain?

Blockchain allows us to create a fair, transparent environment where players are rewarded for their engagement. By utilizing blockchain, we ensure transparency in game mechanics and token distribution.

Why Build on Base?

  • Low Cost: Base offers lower gas fees, making it ideal for frequent transactions.
  • EVM Compatibility: Base is EVM-compatible, allowing for seamless integration with Ethereum tools.
  • Scalability: Base’s infrastructure is built to scale as our platform grows.

Tokenomics

Players earn tokens for winning matches. Over time, we will introduce more sophisticated rewards, including staking and governance participation.

Roadmap

  • Initial Launch: Perfect Rock, Paper, Scissors and integrate token rewards.
  • Expansion: Introduce more casual games such as Tic-Tac-Toe, Higher or Lower, Liar’s Dice, Emoji Guessing Game, 10. Rock-Paper-Scissors-Lizard-Spock
  • Incentivization Upgrades: Develop advanced token reward systems.

Conclusion

Our goal is to build a social gaming experience that combines the fun of casual games with the power of blockchain, creating a sustainable and engaging ecosystem for all users.
